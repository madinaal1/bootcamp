Suki The panda

Suki was my chocolate lab. She was born in 2007 and I got her in 9th grade. She was my favorite puppy ever. She lived a happy life of walks, food, and mischeif. She was a sassy diva, a self-entitled brat, and loved everyone unconditionally. She passed away in 2020 at the age of 13. May she rest in peace. I love you Suki!
